The Magic Paintbrush

A young girl named Mia loved to paint colorful pictures every afternoon. One day, she discovered an old paintbrush hidden inside her grandmother's attic. As soon as she painted a bright red apple, it magically appeared on the table. Excited, Mia painted flowers for the village gardens, books for the school library, and toys for children who had none. She never painted gold or jewels for herself because she believed that happiness grows when it is shared. The villagers thanked her for making their town brighter and kinder. Before disappearing, the magical paintbrush left one final message: the greatest magic is using your talents to help others. Mia smiled, picked up an ordinary brush, and continued creating beautiful art every day.

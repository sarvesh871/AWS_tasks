The Little Cloud That Couldn't Rain

High above the mountains floated a tiny cloud named Puffy. Every cloud in the sky knew how to make rain, but no matter how hard Puffy tried, not a single drop would fall. The other clouds teased him and drifted away to water forests and rivers. Feeling sad, Puffy wandered across the sky until he met a wise old eagle. The eagle smiled and said, "Sometimes the best things happen when we stop worrying." Puffy relaxed and enjoyed watching the world below. As he floated over a dry village, a gentle breeze carried cool air around him. Suddenly, tiny raindrops began to form. Soon, a soft shower watered the thirsty fields, and the children below danced happily in the rain. Puffy realized he had always been capable. He simply needed patience, confidence, and the right moment to shine. From that day onward, whenever the sky turned gray, everyone smiled because they knew Puffy was bringing life to the earth.

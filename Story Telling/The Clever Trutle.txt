The Clever Turtle

A slow-moving turtle dreamed of joining the annual forest race, even though everyone laughed at him. The rabbit, the fox, and the deer all believed they would easily win. When the race began, the faster animals rushed ahead without paying attention to the path. Soon they reached a muddy field and became stuck. The turtle carefully looked around, found a safe trail beside the trees, and slowly continued his journey. Step by step, he crossed the finish line first. The animals congratulated him and apologized for doubting his abilities. The turtle smiled and reminded everyone that patience, careful thinking, and determination are often more valuable than speed. From that day forward, the forest celebrated not just fast runners, but wise ones as well.

The Lost Star

Every night, a tiny star shone brightly in the sky above a peaceful village. One evening, the little star slipped from the clouds and landed in a nearby meadow. A curious boy named Aarav found the glowing star hiding among the flowers. Instead of keeping it as a treasure, he decided to help it return home. Together, they climbed the tallest hill, where the wind gently lifted the star back into the night sky. As the star reached its place, hundreds of sparkling lights appeared around it, making the sky brighter than ever before. The star winked happily at Aarav, reminding him that kindness always finds its way back to the person who shares it.

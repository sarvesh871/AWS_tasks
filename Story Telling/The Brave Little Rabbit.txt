The Brave Little Rabbit

Once upon a time, a little rabbit lived near a quiet forest. Although he was the smallest among all the animals, he always wanted to help others. One morning, he found a baby bird that had fallen from its nest. While the larger animals walked past without noticing, the little rabbit carefully gathered soft leaves, climbed a small hill, and gently returned the bird to its home. The grateful bird sang a cheerful song that echoed through the forest. Soon, every animal learned about the rabbit's kindness. They realized that true courage is not about being the biggest or the strongest. It is about doing the right thing, even when no one is watching. From that day on, the little rabbit became the most respected animal in the forest.
